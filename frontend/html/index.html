Test the cat

Upload a photo of your cat, or any cat you find, and check whether it has orange fur. The image is cropped around the cat so the analysis can focus on the fur instead of the background.

The system uses YOLOv11 from Ultralytics to detect the cat, then OpenCV and NumPy with HSV color rules to estimate the cat's fur color.

The orange cat theory

The Orange Cat Theory, tells all orange cats share a single, collective brain cell. According to the theory, this "lack of brainpower" explains why they are notoriously clumsy, chaotic, and constantly caught doing absurd things or staring blankly into space.

It's not a scientific theory, but rather a collective observation by thousands of cat owners online.
